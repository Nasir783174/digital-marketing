Top 10 Social Media Marketing Agencies in Bangladesh

We ranked the top agencies by real-world results, service depth, and market reputation — so you can stop guessing and find the right growth partner.

By Markedly Editorial 2025 12 min read

Bangladesh has one of the fastest-growing digital markets in South Asia. With over 50 million active Facebook users, the country has become a Facebook-first economy, where brands live or die by their social media presence. Instagram, YouTube, and TikTok are also picking up fast, especially among younger audiences in Dhaka and Chittagong.

But here is the problem: most businesses struggle to run effective social media campaigns. Managing Facebook Ads, creating scroll-stopping content, tracking ROI, and building a consistent brand voice takes time, skill, and experience that most internal teams simply do not have. That is where specialized agencies come in.

Methodology

How We Selected These Agencies

We did not just pull names from a Google search. Our selection process was based on five key factors:

  • Experience in running end-to-end SMM campaigns (not just posting content)
  • Client portfolio — both local Bangladeshi brands and international clients
  • Proven ad performance — do they have case studies or measurable results?
  • Service depth — content creation, paid ads, AND analytics together
  • Market reputation — peer reviews, word-of-mouth, and online presence

Marketorr is a performance-driven digital marketing agency focused on helping SMEs get real, measurable results from Facebook Ads and social media funnels. Decision Guide

Which Agency Should You Choose?

The right agency depends entirely on your business goals, budget, and where you are in your growth journey.

For Startups and Small Businesses

Start with Marketorr or IMBD Agency. Both understand tight budgets and focus on real results. Marketorr is better if paid ads and lead generation are your priority; IMBD is better if you just need consistent content and a basic social media presence.

For Corporate and Enterprise Brands

Magnito Digital and Analyzen are your best bets. Magnito handles large, complex campaigns with multiple stakeholders. Analyzen is the right choice if data, performance measurement, and ROI accountability are your top priorities.

For Creative and Youth-Focused Campaigns

Look at HYPE Dhaka and GEEKY Social. HYPE Dhaka is stronger in brand storytelling; GEEKY Social is better at trend-driven, viral-style content.

For ROI and Performance Advertising

Red Sparrow Digital and Bizcope are the performance specialists. Red Sparrow is pure paid social; Bizcope adds SEO to the mix for long-term organic growth alongside paid campaigns.

The right agency does not just post content for you — they become a growth partner. Choose wisely.
